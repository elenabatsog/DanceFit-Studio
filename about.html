Our Story

DanceFit Studio is a modern gym which offers fitness programs at different levels, dance and aerobic classes during the week. It was created to bring people closer and inspire them to become a better version of themselves. Our goal is to make people happy, satisfy their needs and help them achieve their goals. It is very important to stay active because exercise builds people's confidence and they can find a new hobby. Dancing is a form of art and people can express themselves in the studio.

Apart from the fitness programs and group classes, our team does workshops once a month & DanceFit Studio organizes special events for our community. A member of our gym maybring their friends as well but they need to book in advance.

This website is a form of communication and it is designed as a platform to organize their tasks more efficiently.
